How do we structure the day?

1. Training

We teach all the necessary things to jump from the plane without any kind of problem.

2. Get Ready

You are already prepared, you just need the suit and a parachute. All sizes are available.

3. Fly

You are raedy, and the plane is waiting for us in the hangar. Let's fly!

4. Jump!

You have already done the most complicated parts. Just one step left... jump!
